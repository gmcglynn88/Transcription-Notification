📘 Production Configuration Guide

How It Works (Corrected Two-Stage Architecture)

This app uses a two-stage subscription model:

  1. Stage 1: Subscribe to v2.routing.queues.{queueId}.conversations.calls for each selected queue — fires when a call lands in the queue, providing the conversationId.
  2. Stage 2: Dynamically subscribe to v2.conversations.{conversationId}.transcription for each active conversation to receive real-time transcript segments.

Required Permissions

  • speech-and-text-analytics — For transcription access
  • notifications — For creating channels and WebSocket connections
  • routing:queue:view — For fetching queue list

Transcription Requirements

  • Voice transcription must be enabled in your org
  • Enable per queue: PUT /api/v2/routing/queues/{queueId}/transcription
  • Only Genesys Cloud Voice or BYOC Cloud provide real-time transcription
  • BYOC Premises completes transcription after call ends

API Usage Notes

  • Each notification counts toward your rate limits
  • Channels expire after 24 hours — use Reconnect to recreate
  • WebSocket will auto-reconnect with exponential backoff
  • New conversations are automatically subscribed as they arrive in monitored queues
